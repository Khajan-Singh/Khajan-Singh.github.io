Furthermore, for my Original Work, I am developing an IoT‑based plant monitoring system designed to collect and analyze environmental and soil data to improve crop health and optimize agricultural yield. The system uses interconnected sensors to measure factors such as soil moisture, temperature, and light intensity, transmitting data to a central platform for visualization and analysis. By integrating elements of AI and data modeling, the project aims to detect early signs of plant stress and provide actionable insights for more efficient and sustainable farming practices. This project represents the intersection of my interests in electrical and computer engineering, IoT systems, and environmental innovation.
